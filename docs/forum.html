Since 2009, many posts from ABINIT users have been discussed on the ABINIT forum.
This is an excellent complement to the extensive ABINIT documentation and to the ABINIT wiki.
The forum initially used the "phpBB" technology, and has been transferred to "discourse" beginning of July 2021.
The posts after January 2021 have been duplicated from "phpBB" to "discourse",
while all posts before July 2021 stay accessible read-only on the former platform for the forum.

Please, register to the forum in order to be able to post topics. You will need an invite code.

We are grateful to all contributors, as well as to the moderators.
